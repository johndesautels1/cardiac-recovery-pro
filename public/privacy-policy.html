TL;DR: Your health data stays on YOUR device. We don't collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. You have complete control over your data.

1. Information We Collect

Cardiac Recovery Pro operates as a local-only, privacy-first application. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any of your personal health data on external servers.

The only data that exists is what you voluntarily enter into the app for your own tracking purposes, including:

  • Name (optional)
  • Age, sex, height, weight (for calculations)
  • Surgery date
  • Daily vitals (heart rate, blood pressure, oxygen saturation, etc.)
  • Exercise and therapy session logs
  • Symptoms and notes

2. Data Storage

3. No Account Required

Cardiac Recovery Pro does not require:

  • Account creation
  • Login credentials
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Any identifying information beyond what you choose to enter

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses minimal third-party services:

  • Chart.js CDN: Used to render charts. No personal data is sent.
  • Service Worker: Enables offline functionality. No data transmitted.

No analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising networks are used.

5. Data Export & Backup

You have full control over your data:

  • Export all data as JSON or CSV files anytime
  • Backup to your own device/cloud storage
  • Import data to restore on another device
  • Share with your healthcare team if you choose

6. Data Deletion

You can permanently delete all your data at any time using:

  • "Clear Current Day" - Remove today's entry
  • "Clear All Patient Data" - Remove everything
  • Browser cache clearing
  • Uninstalling the app

7. Geolocation (Optional)

If you enable the GPS tracker feature:

  • Location data is stored locally only
  • Used to tag where exercises occur (home, clinic, etc.)
  • You can disable this feature anytime
  • Never transmitted to servers

8. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for adults (18+) in cardiac recovery programs. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18.

9. HIPAA Compliance

Since no data is transmitted or stored on external servers, HIPAA regulations regarding electronic health records do not apply to this app's operation. However, users should follow their own healthcare provider's guidance on data sharing.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

11. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data (via export feature)
  • Modify your data anytime
  • Delete your data completely
  • Control what data you enter

12. Security

Data security is ensured by:

  • Local-only storage (no network transmission)
  • Browser's built-in security features
  • No login credentials to compromise
  • Recommendation: Use device lock/password

13. Medical Disclaimer

IMPORTANT: Cardiac Recovery Pro is a tracking and informational tool only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult your healthcare provider before starting any exercise program or making medical decisions.
